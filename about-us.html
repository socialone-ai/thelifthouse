Who We Are

Siddham Techno Private Limited, operating under the brand The Lift House, is an established and reputable organization in the elevator industry, with operations spanning since 2011. Headquartered in Jaipur, Rajasthan, the company operates as a B2B trader and manufacturer of elevator components, serving lift manufacturers, assemblers, and distributors across India with a strong commitment to quality, safety, and reliability.

The company functions from an 18,000 sq. ft. integrated facility, encompassing corporate offices, manufacturing units, and warehousing under one roof. This integrated infrastructure supports a comprehensive inventory of 10,000+ lift component SKUs, enabling consistent availability, efficient order fulfilment, and rapid dispatch.

Under the leadership of Mr. Gaurav Jain, Siddham Techno Private Limited continues to pursue sustainable growth with a clear strategic vision—to position itself as India's most dependable one-stop solution provider for lift components and customized elevator solutions, delivering excellence and operational efficiency on a PAN India scale.

What We Do

We deliver end-to-end elevator component solutions through a combination of large-scale trading and in-house manufacturing. Our activities include maintaining ready stock of 10,000+ lift component SKUs for immediate supply, along with the manufacture of customized lift cabins, car frames, counter frames, and door systems in MS, SS, designer, and colour-finish variants.

Through efficient supply chain management, engineering-backed manufacturing, and responsive after-sales support, we enable our customers to execute lift projects with speed, consistency, and confidence across diverse applications.
